# Knowledge

> This folder contains the Living Knowledge System's intellectual artifacts — the owner's evolving understanding of the global financial system.

---

## Structure

```
knowledge/
├── journals/        — Dated personal entries and reflections
├── reflections/     — Longer-form analytical writing
├── observations/    — Short timestamped notices of notable conditions
├── hypotheses/      — Formally stated beliefs under testing
└── case-studies/    — Historical analyses through this system's framework
```

---

## Purpose

These are not reports. They are not research papers. They are not meant for any audience except the owner.

They are the living record of one mind's deepening understanding of the global financial system.

They are the tree rings described in [docs/05-tree-of-understanding.md](../docs/05-tree-of-understanding.md).

---

## How to Use This Folder

Write freely. The purpose is not to produce polished analysis. The purpose is to think.

Over time, patterns will emerge. Old observations will gain new meaning. Hypotheses will be confirmed, revised, or abandoned. Case studies will illuminate the present.

**Never delete entries that turn out to be wrong.** Revise your understanding — but preserve the record of how your thinking has evolved. That evolution is itself knowledge.
