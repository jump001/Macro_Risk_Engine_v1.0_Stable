# Case Studies

> Historical analyses of past episodes of systemic financial stress, examined through this project's framework.

---

## Purpose

Case studies are how the owner learns from history.

The financial system has experienced recurring patterns of stress, crisis, and recovery. Each episode — 2008, 2011, 2013, 2018, 2020, 2022, and beyond — contains lessons about how the transmission chain operates, where the Golden Signal fires, what interventions work, and how the system ultimately resolves.

A case study examines one such episode through the lens of:
- The Tree of Understanding (where was the stress originating?)
- The Financial Medical Record (what were the vital signs?)
- The Risk Engine framework (what Risk State would this have triggered?)
- The Financial Immune System (what treatments were deployed?)

---

## Format

```markdown
# Case Study: [Episode Name and Year]

**Period:** YYYY-MM to YYYY-MM
**Category:** Funding Crisis | Deleveraging | Sovereign Crisis | External Shock | Carry Unwind | Other
**Risk States Reached:** [List from WATCH to DELEVERAGING]
**Golden Signal Fired:** Yes | No | Partial

## Background
[What was the macro environment entering this episode?]

## Transmission Chain
[How did stress propagate through: Environment → Central Bank → Funding → Liquidity → Debt Market → Confidence → Asset Market → Deleveraging]

## Tree Analysis
[Which layer of the tree was most affected? Soil? Roots? Trunk? Branches?]

## Key Indicators
[What did SOFR, DXY, US30Y, Gold, MOVE/VIX, Treasury Auctions show?]

## Medical Record Summary
[Vital signs, diagnosis progression, treatment deployed, response]

## Resolution
[How did the episode resolve? What treatments worked? What remained unresolved?]

## Lessons for This System
[What does this case study teach about recognizing future episodes?]

## What I Understood at the Time vs. Now
[Honest reflection on the difference between understanding then and understanding now]
```

---

## Naming Convention

Files should be named: `CS-NNN-episode-name-year.md`

Example: `CS-001-us-regional-bank-crisis-2023.md`
