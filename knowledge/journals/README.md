# Journals

> Dated personal entries recording observations, thoughts, and reflections on the state of the global financial system.

---

## Purpose

A journal entry is the most free-form artifact in the Living Knowledge System. There are no required fields. There is no required format.

A journal entry might be:

- A short note about something noticed in today's indicators.
- A question that has been sitting in the owner's mind.
- A reaction to something read or heard.
- A connection made between two previously unrelated observations.
- A record of how the owner is *feeling* about what they are seeing — the emotional texture of the diagnosis.

---

## Format (Suggested)

```markdown
# YYYY-MM-DD

## Context
[Optional: what is the current Risk State and notable indicator readings]

## Entry
[Free writing — observations, thoughts, questions]

## Questions
[Open questions to follow up on]
```

---

## Naming Convention

Files should be named by date: `YYYY-MM-DD.md`

Example: `2026-06-27.md`

---

## Principle

> Write even when there is nothing alarming to say.
> Silence in the system is worth recording too.
> "System is calm today. Funding markets quiet. No new stress signals." — that is a valid journal entry.
